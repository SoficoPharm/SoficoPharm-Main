Our Story

SOFICOPHARM Pharmaceuticals S.A.E. is Egypt's first private company in the pharmaceutical and medical industry. Founded by Dr. Maher Scandar — a pioneer who began his career in the 1950s as acting Managing Director of Chemical and Industrial Pharmaceuticals (CID) — the company was built out of a need to introduce important pharmaceutical products to the local market.

With the open-door policy of the late 1970s, Dr. Maher Scandar and Mr. Ezzat Scandar fulfilled their ambition of creating a dedicated pharmaceutical importation and distribution company. Today, SOFICOPHARM remains a proud family business — now led by President Eng. Cherine Scandar, VP & Managing Director Ezzat Scandar, and COO Karim El Ashram — with a team of over 1,500 professionals serving 32,000+ pharmacies, hospitals, and clinics across Egypt.

As sole distributor to leading global companies, SOFICOPHARM provides the market with over 2,000 SKUs from more than 50 international partners, with an annual turnover exceeding $200 million USD.
